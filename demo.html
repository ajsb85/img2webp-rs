Deep Dive: Optimization Matrix

By exploring the WebPConfig headers and the libwebp implementation, we identified several advanced optimization vectors:

  • Alpha Compression Method (alpha_compression): Switches between no compression (0) and lossless compression (1) for the alpha channel. Our tool now exposes this.
  • Alpha Filtering (alpha_filter): 0=None, 1=Fast, 2=Best. "Best" uses complex predictive filtering that significantly improves compression of smooth alpha gradients.
  • Method (-m 0-6): Controls the complexity of the compression search. Level 6 is the "Enterprise Grade" setting for maximum compression, though significantly slower to encode.
  • Sharp YUV (-sharp_yuv): A more accurate RGB to YUV conversion that reduces artifacts at high-contrast edges, specifically targeting "ghosting" or "halos" in lossy animations.
  • Mixed Mode (-mixed): Allows the encoder to decide frame-by-frame whether lossless or lossy is better, often achieving the best quality-to-size ratio.
